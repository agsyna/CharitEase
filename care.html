About US
Charlies Animal Rescue Centre-CARE is an animal shelter established in January 2013, with the aim of providing timely medical aid to injured and ill animals of the street in Bangalore city.
No animal left behind is our motto.CARE is also home to many dogs,cats, rabbits,ducks, hens, pigs, guinea pigs, birds and terrapins.
The inspiration behind the trust, is 15 year old Charlie, a differently abled, 3 legged Indian Dog who was an established figure in the field of canine therapy since 2005.

Why Join Us
Join CARE to make a tangible difference in the lives of animals. Be part of a compassionate community dedicated to providing love, care, and medical aid to those in need. Gain valuable experience in animal care and rescue operations, and help educate the community about animal welfare. Your efforts will directly impact the well-being of injured, ill, and abandoned animals, giving them a second chance at happiness.
