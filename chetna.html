About US
At Chetna NGO, we are passionately committed to transforming the lives of street and working children in India. Since our inception in 2002, we have focused on providing education, vocational training, and healthcare to empower these vulnerable children. Our mission is to ensure that every child we reach can realize their full potential, live with dignity, and enjoy the rights they deserve. Through our efforts, we aim to create lasting change in their lives and in the communities they come from.
